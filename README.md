## Description

Eat the Burger is objectively the most delicious app on the web (according to burger experts, but mostly myself). These days, burgers come in all shapes and sizes. Most have lettece or cheese, but few are made out of pixels. Eat the Burger provides the user with an immersive, interactive experience where they can create their own custom burger and devour it!

![eat the burger website](/readmeImages/website.png)

## Instructions

1. Start by entering your preferred burger name into the burger form entry box and click Submit.

![burger entry box](/readmeImages/burger-entry.png)

2. Once submitted, your burgers will show up in the "Ready-to-Eat" tray. 

![ready to eat burgers tray](/readmeImages/ready-to-eat.png)

3. Click Devour to consume the delicious, pixel burger.

4. After devouring, your burgers will show up in the Burgers Eaten tray.

![consumed burgers tray](/readmeImages/consumed.png)

5. To clear out the Burgers Eaten tray, click the Reset button.

*Fun Fact! The total amount of burgers eaten is cumulative with other users. You may see burgers that others have eaten in the Ready-to-Eat tray or Burgers Eaten tray.*
